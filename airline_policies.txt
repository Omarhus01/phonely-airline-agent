PHONELY AIR POLICIES
====================

CANCELLATION AND REFUNDS
Cancellations made within 24 hours of booking: full refund, no questions asked.
Cancellations made between 24 and 72 hours before departure: 50% refund.
Cancellations made less than 24 hours before departure: no refund.
No-shows (failure to cancel and failure to board): no refund.
Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days to the original payment method.

FLIGHT CHANGES
Passengers may change their flight once at no charge if the change is made more than 48 hours before departure.
Changes made within 48 hours of departure are subject to a $75 change fee.
If the new flight is more expensive, the passenger pays the fare difference in addition to any applicable change fee.
Flight changes can be requested by calling customer support.

BAGGAGE
One carry-on bag is included free of charge for all passengers.
The first checked bag costs $35.
The second checked bag costs $50.
Bags exceeding 50 pounds are subject to an overweight fee of $75.
Bags exceeding 62 inches in total dimensions are subject to an oversized fee of $100.
Phonely Air is not responsible for fragile or perishable items placed in checked luggage.

LOST OR DELAYED BAGGAGE
Passengers must file a lost baggage claim within 24 hours of arrival at any Phonely Air service desk or by calling customer support.
Phonely Air will reimburse up to $500 for essential items purchased while baggage is delayed more than 12 hours.
If baggage is confirmed lost, Phonely Air will reimburse up to $1,500 for the contents.

SEAT SELECTION
Standard seat selection is free during booking.
Extra legroom seats are available for $25 to $50 depending on the route.
Seat upgrades to business class are subject to availability and priced per route.

SPECIAL ASSISTANCE
Passengers requiring wheelchair assistance or special accommodations must notify Phonely Air at least 48 hours before departure by calling customer support.
Traveling with pets is allowed in the cabin for a $95 fee per flight for animals under 20 pounds in an approved carrier.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT
Customer support is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
Callers can ask to be transferred to a customer support agent at any time during this call.
